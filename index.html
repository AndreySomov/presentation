Imagine that our company sells killer-robots.

We want to understand what people think about us

For this we need:

  1. Collect data for the training sample: we denote each comment as 'x and its emotional color as 'y' positive or negative
  2. Develop our model, which we will apply to our data, call it 'a (x)', and train it.
  3. Apply our model to comments from social networks in which we are mentioned.

Comment examples

Thank you very much! This is exactly what I was looking for, the quality is excellent! My neighbors will no longer bother me. The only negative is that it burned the floor of the area, with my house too.

Terrible quality! All the Gypsies of the camp were thrown off to him, but he does not understand that we order him, I advise you to avoid this manufacturer. Money back and thanks for that!

Learning algorithm(optimizer)

Full algorithm

  • Collect training data
  • Create a bag of words(tags)
  • Determine approximate weights for our tags
  • Apply loss function
  • Apply learning algorithm(optimizer)
  • Repeat the last 2 steps until we get the desired accuracy.
  • Apply the model on comments from social networks
